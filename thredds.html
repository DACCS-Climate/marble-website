THREDDS Data Server

The goal of the Thematic Real-time Environmental Distributed Data Services (THREDDS) project is to simplify the discovery and use of scientific data.

Through the management of metadata and data access to scientific datasets, THREDDS allows scientific publications and educational materials to reference scientific data. Let us worry about the data storage while you carry your work with you.
